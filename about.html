About ZedAds

Built by Zambians, for Zambians — connecting communities across all 10 provinces.

Who We Are

ZedAds is Zambia's leading multi-category advertising platform, designed to bridge the gap between service providers, sellers, and buyers across the country. Whether you're in Lusaka, Kitwe, Livingstone, or Chipata — ZedAds makes it easy to reach your audience.

We understand the Zambian market and its unique needs. Our platform is built with local pricing (in Kwacha), local geography, and local business culture in mind. No complicated processes — just post, browse, and connect.

Our Mission

To empower Zambian entrepreneurs, businesses, and individuals by providing an accessible, affordable, and effective advertising platform that stimulates local commerce and economic growth.
